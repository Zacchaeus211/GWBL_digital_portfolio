About the Department

Group Operations & Technology (O&T) is the engine room of the OCBC ecosystem, split across two mandates: running the bank today, and building what runs it tomorrow.

Run the Bank

Group Operations

The regional processing hubs. They handle the day-to-day volume — transactions, corporate lending pipelines, and back-end processing across markets — while constantly refining workflows to cut cost and friction.

Change the Bank

Group Technology

The digital architects. They build and scale the bank's digital backbone: mobile banking, AI and data analytics platforms, and the cybersecurity infrastructure that protects it all, 24/7.

My Team: Operations Change Management

When Technology ships a new tool or automates a process, a bank can't just flip a switch. My team is the bridge that makes the rollout safe.

Smooth Transitions

Mapping exactly how new technology — like automated AI document processors — reshapes human workflows, so the switch happens with zero disruption to daily operations or clients.

Risk & Process Alignment

Updating regional SOPs and making sure new processes stay strictly compliant with financial regulation and risk frameworks.

Workforce Enablement

Coordinating across business units to train and prepare staff so they're confident using the bank's next-generation platforms.

How My Role Works

I'm still early in this internship, so I don't have one fixed "role" or "project" — my job is to support the Operations Change Management team with whatever they need next, which shifts week to week or month to month. Some weeks that's writing SQL, some weeks it's cleaning and documenting a data pipeline, and increasingly it's starting to include data visualization in Power BI. The Evidence of Work page reflects what I've been assigned so far — it will keep growing and changing shape as the internship continues.
